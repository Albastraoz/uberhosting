About us

The beginning

Uber hosting began in 2012 as a small company which set up websites for customers in Ireland with the focus on small businesses. Later we in 2014 we started delivering our services in the UK and France. In 2016 we began with hosting servers for clients all over europe and today our services are available all over the world.

Our aim

The main focus is to deliver a service with a very high security and easy to use user interaction. Over the years we created a solid CMS system for our users where they can easy host websites, manage email addresses and create beautifull apps.

Customer support

Where you have problems deploying your websites or apps we come with solutions! Our support team with years of experience in software development are available to you every day and will help you all the way. Send us an email through our contact form and a developer will contact you as soon as possible.

Future

Our future plans are to increase support for the asian market with support in multiple languages like chinese and japanese. We already have offices in America and Europe and will soon continue with new offices in Africa and Australia.
